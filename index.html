Our Story
Rooted in
Lakeland

ONE Radio is Lakeland's premier independent radio station, proudly serving Polk County and all of Central Florida from our home in the 33811 zip code.

We play today's biggest hits alongside the best of the '90s and 2000s — a mix that keeps the whole family tuned in. From morning commutes on US-98 to weekend cookouts, ONE Radio is the soundtrack of your life in Lakeland.

Community matters to us. We spotlight local events, Lakeland High athletics, and Central Florida artists who deserve to be heard. This is your station.
